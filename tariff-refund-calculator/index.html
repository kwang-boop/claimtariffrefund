Understanding the IEEPA tariff rates by country.

Country IEEPA Rate Basis Notes

Important limitations

This calculator provides rough estimates only. Your actual refund depends on specific HS codes, the exact tariff classification of each entry, liquidation status, and whether Post-Summary Corrections or protests were filed. For accurate figures, run the ES-003 report in your ACE account.
